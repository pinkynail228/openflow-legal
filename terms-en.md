OpenFlow `0.1.0-beta.2` is provided as a free Mermaid-first Community beta.

revocable invite key and uses limited shared capacity. This is not a production
service or a guarantee of uninterrupted availability.

Invite keys are intended for their recipient and may be revoked. Do not publish,
sell, or share a key. Anti-abuse limits include at most 100 upstream requests per
UTC day; repair and format fallback calls also consume this limit.

OpenFlow is intended for user-flow design in Figma Design. Automated relay abuse,
limit bypass, credential extraction, malicious use, and infringement of third-party
rights are prohibited. Do not submit secrets or sensitive personal data.

AI output and Mermaid source may be incomplete or incorrect. Users must review
Preview before Apply and are responsible for the resulting diagram. The beta is
provided “as is” to the extent permitted by applicable law.
